League Of Legends

Bracket Teams

Description

To sign up, you need to make sure your email address is verified, and either create a team, or join an existing team (the team captain can send you an invite from the team page). Only team captains can register a team for the tournament. To be eligible, a team must have at least 5 players, all of whom must have added their League of Legends IGNs to their profile. The full rules and code of conduct for the tournament can be found here. Standings for each week will be updated in this spreadsheet.

Key Information

Key Tournament Information:

Format: Swiss League, then Playoffs

Platform: PC

Team size: 5 (+ subs)

Length: 8 weeks (see below for schedule)

Signups Close: Monday 8th February

Tournament Starts: Wednesday 10th February

Welcome Summoners, to the Spring season of the UK's premier League of Legends University Tournament!

Show your skills against university teams from around the UK!

This tournament will follow a Swiss League system for the first five weeks, designed to give everybody a chance of winning while still matching teams as closely as possible.

All teams will finish the tournament with elimination playoffs against the teams closest to them to determine final tournament placement.

All abilities are welcome!

Join the NUEL Discord to meet other players, attend captains meetings, receive on-the-night admin support and stay up to date with tournament information/updates.

Sign up using this form!
