Welcome Agents, to the Spring Split of The NUEL's Clash Royale University Tournament!

Show your skills against university teams from around the UK!

This is a 1v1 format that will see you pitted against other university students from across the UK. Players will be given points based on their finish position each week. The player with the most points at the end of the season will claim the title of best university Clash Royale player, as well as picking up some prizes along the way.

Points Per Week: 1st: 500pts, 2nd: 350pts, 3rd: 200pts, 4th: 160pts, 5th: 130pts, 7th: 100pts, 9th: 70pts, 13th: 40pts, 17th: 20pts, 25th: 10pts, 33rd+: 5pts

The tournament is open to all abilities!

Broadcast: Live every tournament night on The Nuel

Join the NUEL Discord to meet other players, attend captains meetings, receive on-the-night admin support and stay up to date with tournament information/updates.
