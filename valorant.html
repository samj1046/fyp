Valorant

Bracket Teams

Description

Only team captains can register a team for the tournament. To be eligible, a team must have at least 5 players, all of whom must have added their Valorant IGNs to their profile.

The full rules and code of conduct for the tournament can be found here.

The standings for the season can be found here and it will be updated soon after all games finish for the night.

Key Information

Key Tournament Information:

Format: Swiss League, then Playoffs

Platform: PC

Team size: 5 (+ subs)

Length: 8 weeks (see right for schedule)

Signups Close: Friday 5th February

Tournament Starts: Sunday 7th February

Welcome Agents, to the Spring Split of The NUEL's Valorant University Tournament!

Show your skills against university teams from around the UK!

This tournament will follow a Swiss League system for the first five weeks, designed to give everybody a chance of winning while still matching teams as closely as possible.

All teams will finish the tournament with elimination playoffs against the teams closest to them to determine final tournament placement.

The tournament is open to all abilities!

Join the NUEL Discord to meet other players, attend captains meetings, receive on-the-night admin support and stay up to date with tournament information/updates.
